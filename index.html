Ctrl.Alt.Connect Reel Template System

Three brands, each with 8 reel formats built from the same Cover, Content and Outro frames. Pick a brand and a format, then press Play. Toggle the safe zones to check nothing sits under the TikTok or Instagram controls.

Safe zones: both apps cover the bottom (caption and username) and the action rail on the right. Keep text and logos inside the green box. Instagram's band is the taller of the two, so if it fits Instagram it fits TikTok.
